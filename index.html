Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your hosting package?

Our enterprise hosting includes SSL certificates, daily backups, 24/7 monitoring, DDoS protection, and CDN integration. We guarantee 99.9% uptime and provide regular security updates.

How long does it take to complete a website project?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks. Simple websites can be done in as little as 1 week, while more complex e-commerce sites may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

What ongoing support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support. Our team is available via phone, email, and chat during business hours.

How do you handle revisions and changes?

Our process includes two rounds of revisions at no additional cost. We work closely with you to ensure the final product matches your vision perfectly.

What makes your SEO services different?

We implement advanced SEO strategies including schema markup, local SEO optimization, and mobile-first indexing. Our approach has helped clients achieve top rankings for competitive keywords.
